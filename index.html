How long will it take to build a website

The time depends on the type and complexity of the website. A basic site with a few pages can take about 1 to 2 weeks, while larger or more complex sites like e-commerce platforms or custom web applications can take 3 to 6 weeks or more. I always provide a clear timeline before starting, and I work efficiently to meet your deadlines without compromising quality.

How much will it take to build a website

The cost varies based on your needs. Simple websites start at an affordable rate, while custom features like login systems, payment gateways, dashboards, and responsive animations will increase the price. Once I understand your requirements, I’ll send you a free quote with no hidden charges. My goal is to give you real value for your investment.

will my website work on phones and laptops

Yes, absolutely. All websites I build are fully responsive, meaning they look great and function smoothly on desktops, tablets, and mobile phones. Today, over 70% of traffic comes from mobile users,I make sure your site is mobile-friendly from the ground up.
